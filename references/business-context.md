# Business Context: The Brotherhood Therapy

**Source:** https://www.thebrotherhood.ca/
**Last updated:** 2026-05-15

---

## Business Fundamentals

- **Name:** The Brotherhood Therapy
- **Business Type:** Men's mental health therapy practice
- **Service Model:** 100% online psychotherapy and counselling

## Services Offered

- Individual psychotherapy sessions ($185/hour)
- Free initial consultation
- **Specializations:** emotional regulation, trauma, relationship difficulties, addiction, life transitions, self-esteem, couples therapy
- **Evidence-based approaches:** DBT, EMDR, and parts-based therapy

## Target Market

Men seeking therapy who are experiencing:
- Emotional dysregulation
- Relationship challenges
- Recurring negative patterns
- Life transitions

The practice targets men ready for "real change" rather than surface-level discussion.

## Location & Service Area

- **Geographic Focus:** Ontario (all therapists licensed in Ontario)
- **Service Delivery:** Fully virtual/online platform

## Unique Value Propositions

- Exclusive focus on men's mental health
- "Practical, direct therapy" approach emphasizing accountability and application over theory
- Therapist team with lived experience in trauma recovery and personal transformation
- Structured session framework: clarify, connect patterns, address root issues

## Brand Voice & Key Messaging

- **Tone:** Direct, honest, no-nonsense, action-oriented
- **Core Message:** "Therapy should be practical, honest, and create real change"
- **Emphasis:** Progress, effort, and integrity matter; engagement required
